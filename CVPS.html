Step 1: UK Job Search

Before you write your application, you need to find the right opportunities. Explore these popular UK graduate job boards.

  • MyCareer Jobs Board - Exclusive jobs for UoS students.
  • Bright Network
  • TARGETjobs
  • Gradcracker (STEM roles)
  • RateMyPlacement

Step 2: Prepare Your Story (CV)

Your CV is more than a document; it's your professional story. Use these resources to build a compelling CV that grabs an employer's attention.

  • CV Booster Pathway on MyCareer - A step-by-step online guide to writing a professional CV that meets UK standards.
  • Book a CV Support Appointment - Get 1-to-1 feedback on your CV from a careers advisor.

Step 3: Write Your Application

Tailor your application to each role by writing effective cover letters and personal statements. Using the STAR method is key to demonstrating your skills with evidence.

Cover Letters & Personal Statements

  • Access our online guide to writing impactful cover letters.
  • Book an appointment to get your draft application checked.

The STAR Framework (Situation, Task, Action, Result)

This is the gold standard for providing evidence of your skills in applications and interviews.

  • Prospects guide to Competency Based Interviews
  • Indeed guide to using STAR

Step 4: Ace Your Interview

Proper preparation is the key to interview success. Use these tools to practice and build your confidence.

  • E-learning module "Interview Success" - Highly Recommended! Watch videos of good and bad answer examples.
  • Sign up for Interviews Workshops - Practice your skills in an interactive group setting.
  • Book an Ask the Adviser appointment - Run through a mock interview or ask specific questions.

Stuck or Have Questions?
